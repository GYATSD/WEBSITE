About Rush Tag

Rush Tag is an immersive multiplayer game where speed, strategy, and agility are key. Players race around dynamic arenas, tag each other to score points, and compete to be the fastest. Whether you're playing solo or with friends, Rush Tag offers endless fun and excitement!

Key Features:

  • Fast-Paced Gameplay: Sprint, jump, and tag your way through thrilling arenas.
  • Multiple Arenas: Each game takes place in a different dynamic environment.
  • Multiplayer Mode: Play with friends or challenge strangers to become the ultimate Rush Tag champion.
  • Leaderboards: Compete to make it to the top of the global leaderboard.
  • Join the Rush Tag Community: Connect with fellow players through our Discord server.

Gameplay Mechanics:

In Rush Tag, players are divided into two teams or compete individually to tag each other in various arenas.
